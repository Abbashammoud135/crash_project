The player controls Crash, a genetically enhanced bandicoot created by the mad scientist Doctor Neo Cortex.

Description

The game Crash is famous among players for its simple and engaging gameplay. It is mostly preferred by fast-paced games lovers. The concept is pretty simple.... The players are given time to place bets and after the betting period expires, the odds progressively grow until the growth vector randomly crashes. That is called “Crash”. Hence, the players need to cash out before the multiplier stops and get their winnings. Results are calculated by multiplying the bet amount and the odds the user cashed out with. Besides the extreme excitement the game gives, the fair results are guaranteed, too. Each outcome is predefined by RNG which makes the process completely transparent for your users. The players are provided with the coded game result 5 rounds prior.
